About Silverpiston:

Established in 2020, Silver Piston is the strategic capability center for Daily Food Company, a house of leading QSR brands in the GCC. At the core, Silver Piston as a strategic, operational, and implementation advisor enables the business to accelerate its growth, performance improvement, and value delivery process by providing real-time insights and executable solutions that are fostered by an action-oriented approach. Leveraging integrated tools such as automation, advanced analytics based on business intelligence, and focused projects, we equip the company to consistently deliver strong outcomes not just across a wide range of verticals but countries as well.

Daily Food Co. owns and operates multiple QSR brands, including Maestro Pizza (leading pizza chain in KSA & UAE), Pinzatta (ghost kitchens for gourmet pizzas), MAD Pizza (Detroit-style pizzas), and Groundr (burger brand), spanning across 200+ locations with an annual turnover of $100 Mn. The company started its journey in 2013 with the launch of Maestro in KSA and quickly achieved country-wide leadership within just 4 years of operations. Eleven years after the beginning, they launched new brands (Pinzatta, MAD & Groundr) and expanded into a new geography (UAE) with aspirations to build and grow multiple other brands & locations under its aegis. With continued top-line and strong bottom-line growth, the company has created shareholder value and amassed strong investor confidence, putting it on an ambitious track in the coming years. A total of around 3,000 associates have helped shape this story — which was shortlisted by a top-ranked US business school for a strategic case in their MBA program (Maestro Pizza: Coming in hot!).
